## 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- Cross-platform YouTube playback (Windows, macOS, Linux, ChromeOS, Android, iOS, web)
  via `youtube_explode_dart` stream resolution + `media_kit` (libmpv) playback.
- `UniversalYoutubePlayer` widget and `UniversalYoutubeController`.
- Quality selection (low/medium/high/best), live (HLS) support, and metadata.
